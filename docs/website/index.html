About SPEEM

Welcome to SPEEM, the Serverless Private Encrypted End-to-End Messaging app designed with your privacy in mind. SPEEM ensures that your communications are secure and private, without relying on centralized servers.

Our Mission

SPEEM aims to provide a truly private messaging experience. By eliminating the need for centralized servers, we ensure that your data remains in your hands. Our innovative architecture allows for peer-to-peer communication, encrypted messaging, and secure key management all within the app itself.

Key Features

  • Fully serverless architecture for maximum privacy.
  • End-to-end encryption to secure your messages.
  • Peer-to-peer messaging without centralized servers.
  • Secure key generation and storage on your device.
  • User pairing through QR codes or local network discovery.

Roadmap

We are currently in the development phase and are working towards our Proof of Concept (PoC) version. Our roadmap includes:

  • PoC Version: Basic functionalities including secure registration, key generation, and peer-to-peer messaging.
  • Version 1 (V1): Enhanced features like message queuing, push notifications, user presence, and decentralized discovery.
  • Future Versions: Continuous improvements based on user feedback, enhanced security protocols, and more features to ensure the best private messaging experience.
